Lexus IS300

Lexus originated from a corporate project to develop a new premium sedan, code-named F1, which began in 1983. The lexus IS300 is an extremly well-developed car. In 2001, Lexus wanted the IS300 to be an extrovert and an athlete. It was their take at the "sporty sedan" car scence which was dominated by it's German rivals.

  • Below is the video that inspired me to buy the car. I was originally going to buy another car, but this caught my eye. It has a lot of history and it is very reliable. It has very sleek lines makes driving enjoyable

Specifications

Here are a few aspects of the car that every petrolhead must know-

  • 3.0L I6 (2JZ GE)
  • 0-60 mph: 7.5s
  • Fuel Economy: 16/21 mpg
  • Warranty: 4 year/50,000 mile
  • Drive train: RWD

Features

The IS300 comes stock with many modern-day features:

  • Heated Seats
  • Sports/Snow mode
  • Navigation
  • 6 CD Changer
  • Drive train: RWD
